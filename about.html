ABOUT ME

With so much information out there about what hiring managers & recruiters are looking for in potential candidates, it can be understandably hard to figure out where to even begin. I've worked in multiple industries since graduating from Washington State University in 2012 with a degree in Social Sciences, but not before enduring thousands of job applications and hundreds of in-person and phone interviews. Rather than continue to apply and never hear back, I decided to do everything I could to get the upper hand on this process. Now years later, I want to use my experiences, education, as well as my personal connections to help you job search smarter and not harder.

I would primarily work with you remotely to accommodate with your daily schedule but I also make myself available for face to face coaching sessions as well. I don't believe in overpromising and underdelivering. While I cannot promise you that I will immediately find you a new position, I can promise you that our partnership will not end until you're hired into a new position. I look forward to sharing my experiences and the lessons learned with you as well as offering you my committed support. Support with the job search, reviewing and editing your resume & cover letter, your online presence, and supporting you with tips for proper health & overall wellness.

Get in touch with me at zack@careerrefined.com or fill out our contact form.
